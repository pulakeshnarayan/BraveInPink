Brave in Pink

Our Story

Brave in Pink was born out of compassion, unity, and the desire to create lasting change. As a team of young individuals deeply affected by the struggles of breast cancer in our community, we felt the urgent need to step up. Our mission is more than just raising awareness — it’s about amplifying voices, honoring fighters and survivors, and building real connections across North Carolina. Together, we’re committed to hosting impactful events, supporting local organizations, and igniting hope. Brave in Pink isn’t just a cause — it’s a movement, and we’re just getting started.
